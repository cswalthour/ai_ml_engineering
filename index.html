SUMMARY:

The AI/ML Patterns team at 84.51 is designing and developing best-practice patterns and tooling for AI and ML applications. We synthesize the latest and best in AI and ML, build foundational tooling and reusable patterns, and drive adoption by technical users to transform the Kroger enterprise.

As a Sr. AI Engineer on the AI/ML Patterns team, you will be an expert in GenAI end Embeddings patterns . You will become an expert in developing Reusable Science Patterns to accelerate the release of and promote best practices in AI systems like agents and pre-defined architectures like retrieval-augmented generation (RAG). You will leverage your expertise to understand and align on development requirements, consult with internal AI experts on best practices, develop reusable patterns, and upskill the team on developed patterns. Partnering with the other members of the team, you will work to maintain up-to-date knowledge on AI patterns and workflows. This role requires a and excellent software development skills.

You will be successful if you develop patterns that help data scientists and engineers generate new value, develop their projects faster, promote best practices, and reduce risk in AI and ML systems.
